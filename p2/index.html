Project 2 stores

1. Grindline Supply Co. (Skate Shop)

An online skate store that sells custom decks, trucks, wheels, and streetwear inspired by local skate culture. The site features a clean, gritty layout with motion clips of real skaters testing the gear. Includes a “Build Your Setup” tool where users can customize and preview their board before checkout.

2. Bloom & Byte (Eco Tech Accessories)

A shop that blends sustainability with design—selling biodegradable phone cases, solar chargers, and recycled-material laptop sleeves. The brand identity mixes natural textures with sleek digital design, highlighting eco-conscious innovation.

3. Ember Studio (Home Fragrance & Decor)

A minimalist online boutique offering artisan candles, incense, and home accents. The site uses soft animations, warm tones, and scent-based categories (“Wood,” “Citrus,” “Smoke”) for a sensory-inspired browsing experience.
